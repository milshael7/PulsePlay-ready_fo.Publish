PulsePlay Platform Rules & Regulations

1. Marketplace & Selling
	•	PulsePlay does not allow the sale of branded products.
	•	Users who choose to sell branded products do so at their own risk.
	•	Other products must comply with all local, state, and country laws.
	•	Attempting to sell counterfeit, stolen, or illegal products may result in account suspension or permanent removal.

2. Videos & Live Streaming
	•	All videos and live streams must be legal, safe, and appropriate.
	•	Content including nudity, sexual acts, harassment, threats, or illegal activity will result in immediate blocking, suspension, or permanent removal.
	•	Users are responsible for their content and any consequences from posting it.

3. Platform Usage & Interaction
	•	Users must not attempt to access restricted areas (e.g., admin, manager, or backend pages).
	•	All interactions with other members must be respectful and safe.
	•	Violations may result in temporary or permanent suspension, depending on severity.

4. News Feed & Community Content
	•	All posts, comments, and shared content must be appropriate and follow the law.
	•	PulsePlay is not liable for user-generated content.
	•	Misuse of the news feed, harassment, or spamming may result in account restrictions, temporary bans, or permanent removal.

5. General Conduct & Responsibility
	•	All activities on PulsePlay are at the user’s own risk.
	•	Violating rules may lead to account suspension or permanent removal, depending on severity.
	•	Users must follow all applicable local, state, and country laws while using the platform.
	•	Continued use of PulsePlay after updates to these rules constitutes acceptance of the updated rules.
